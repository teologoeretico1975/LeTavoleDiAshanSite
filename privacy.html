Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Le Tavole di Ashan is developed and published by Percy Studio. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our mobile application.

Information We Do Not Collect

Le Tavole di Ashan does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any contact information.
  • We do not collect your location data.
  • We do not collect device identifiers for advertising purposes.
  • We do not use analytics services or tracking SDKs.
  • We do not share any data with third parties.

Data Stored on Your Device

The application saves your game progress locally on your device. This includes:

  • Completed levels and star ratings
  • In-game currency (coins) balance
  • Language preference
  • Daily puzzle completion records
  • Diary fragments you have unlocked

This data is stored exclusively on your device using AES-256 encryption. It is never transmitted to any server. If you uninstall the application, this data is permanently deleted.

In-App Purchases

Le Tavole di Ashan offers an optional in-app purchase to unlock the full game. This transaction is handled entirely by Google Play. Percy Studio does not receive, store, or process any payment information. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details on how Google handles purchase data.

Children's Privacy

Our application does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. As we collect no personal data from any user, no special provisions are required.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the application after any changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

Governing Law
